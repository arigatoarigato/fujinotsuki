You can experience original potteries making!

　 You can enjoy the electric potter's wheel at the historic pottery workshop. Professional potters will gently and politely support and supervise your work, so anyone can participate with confidence and without fear! The finished work will be mailed to your home at a later date. Why don't you make your own original pottery as a memory of your trip?

Date and time：Every Saturday, Sunday and public holidays from 11:00
Fee：Elementary school students 1000yen
Junior higl school students and above 2000yen
