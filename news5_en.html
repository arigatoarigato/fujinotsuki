日本語

We will hold a tasting party for the new Sparkling wine, called "Fujǐno Tskǐno Uā Umāin".

The "Fujǐno Tskǐno Uā Umāin" Sparkling wine, that has been newly added as a specialty in the Fujinotsuki town, is accomplished with the collaboration of a Sake brewery called 'Shigematsu-Shuzo',that was founded in the 3rd year of the Bunsei period(year 1820), and a new wine producing company called 'Fujitsuki Tenjin', which has achieved a rapid growth in recent years.
This alcohol, which benefits from the use of only high quality grapes, cultivated in the Fujinotsuki town, is expected to be highly evaluated not only in Japan but also overseas in the future.
How about being one of the first people who will experience this mellow aroma and fruity sweetness ?

Date and time：28/1/2021（Thu）19:00～
Fee：3000 yen
