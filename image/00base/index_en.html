HOW TO ACCESS

By Car

  • Get off the Fujinotsuki ramp on National Highway 303 about 10km from Akasaka.
  • Get off the Fujinotsuki ramp on Kyushu Expressway about 45km from Shirogane.
    • [Jump to Google Maps]

By Train

  • From Hakata Station
    Take the Shigematsu Line of JR kyushu from Hakata Station to Fujinotsuki Station and 1 minite on foot.
  • From Tenjin
    Take the Fukuoka Airport Line of Fukuoka City Subway from Tenjin Station and switch the Shigematsu Line of JR kyushu at Hakata Station, get off Fujinotsuki Station. 1 minite on foot from the station.
