Soothing SUP
in a beautiful lake

SUP is the abbreviation of "Stand up Paddleboard", it is a watersport that has received a great attention. While training on an exclusive board with high floating power, you can take delight in the beautiful view of the Fujimi lake.
You can also rent all the required equipment for SUP. There will be more than 2 professional lecturers that will support and guide you, so you can participate without hesitation.
How about from enjoying a special time away from everyday life ?

Period：Early Jun to late September
Fee：Elementary school students 1500yen
Junior higl school students and above 2000yen

◇Reservations can be made by phone.
　※Reservation required until the prior day
　※Please avoid cancelling without permission.
